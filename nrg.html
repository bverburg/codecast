NRG
The NRG framework offers the possibility to link your business process and an Enterprise Service BUS. Possibly combined with your current backend system.
This unique combination offers you the possibility to fully control the automation of your processes. You yourself control what processes are going to be automated and to what extent. You retain the ownership of your processes yourself and, if you allow it, maintain your own current back-end system. This ensures a simple transition and low business impact.
