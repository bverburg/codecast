PowerFlow

PowerFlow offers an integrated solution of business processes, an Enterprise Service Bus and the Nexant Revenue Manager billing system for the Dutch energy market.
A comprehensive web interface on a secure environment provides insight into all ongoing and completed processes as well as a task list where users and team leads can assign, pick up and complete any manual tasks.
Keeping Revenue Manager up-to-date and other tasks such as sending e-mails, processing meter readings or customer invoices will automatically take PowerFlow from the business process.

Would you like to know more about the possibilities that PowerFlow offers? Do not hesitate to contact us.
